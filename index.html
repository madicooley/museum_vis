Museum+Vis

The secret life of museum artifacts

Welcome to Museum Vis. Museum Vis is an interactive visualization that you can use to explore the provenance of museum art collections. For this project we individually acquired and processed art collection data from seven different museums in North America.

This visualization led to many interesting findings. For example, MoMA had a fire in 1958. The visualization shows the negative effect this had on the collection up until the 80s. Click the visualization button to see more.
